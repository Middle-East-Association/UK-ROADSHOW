Gateway to MENA:
Unlocking Business Opportunities

The MEA UK Roadshow is a dynamic multi-city business series connecting UK companies with one of the world's fastest-growing and most opportunity-rich regions.

Each event brings together senior business leaders, investors, Government representatives, professional services firms, and regional experts for a focused programme covering trade, investment, market entry, and commercial growth across the Gulf and wider Middle East.

Whether you are already exploring the region or simply curious to understand the opportunities emerging across MENA, this event offers a unique chance to gain practical insight from organisations actively operating in the market.

What You'll Gain

  • Learn about real commercial opportunities across the Gulf and wider MENA region
  • Gain practical market-entry insights from experienced operators and advisers
  • Understand the legal, financial, cultural and strategic aspects of doing business in the region
  • Build connections with businesses, investors, advisers and Government stakeholders
  • Participate in pre-bookable one-to-one meetings with speakers and partners

The Middle East Association

Building bridges between the UK and the Middle East

The Middle East Association (MEA) is a UK organisation dedicated to fostering vital connections and informed dialogue between the UK and the Middle East and North Africa (MENA). Since 1961, we've served as a trusted bridge, uniting governments, businesses, academic institutions, and civil society across political, economic, and cultural spheres.

Through a diverse and dynamic programme of high-level events, the MEA provides essential platforms for discussion, collaboration, and relationship-building.

Our activities range from flagship receptions and gala dinners to private briefings and cultural events, all designed to facilitate constructive exchange and practical outcomes.
